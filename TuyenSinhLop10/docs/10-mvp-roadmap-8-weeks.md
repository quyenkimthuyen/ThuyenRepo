# MVP Roadmap 8 Weeks

## Week 1: Product Foundation

- Chot PRD, taxonomy mon hoc, ma tran de thi.
- Thiet ke database va API contract.
- Khoi tao repo Next.js/TypeScript/Tailwind.
- Tieu chi xong: dashboard placeholder, auth flow mock, docs duoc review.

## Week 2: Content va Question Bank

- Import 2.100 cau seed.
- Xay trang topic list, question list, practice session co ban.
- Admin import JSONL va validation.
- Tieu chi xong: hoc sinh lam duoc cau hoi, hien loi giai.

## Week 3: Practice Engine

- Cham cau tu dong, luu attempt, favorite, wrong answer.
- Spaced repetition co ban.
- Bo loc theo mon/topic/muc do.
- Tieu chi xong: cau sai tu dong vao lich on lai.

## Week 4: Exam Mode

- Exam templates Toan, Van, Anh theo cau truc TP.HCM.
- Timed exam, submit, score report.
- Tieu chi xong: chay duoc 1 de mo phong moi mon.

## Week 5: AI Tutor

- AI gateway, prompt modes, context retrieval.
- Tutor trong man practice.
- Guardrail cho exam mode.
- Tieu chi xong: AI goi y tung buoc va khong lo dap an trong mode tu luyen.

## Week 6: Essay Grading va Study Plan

- Cham bai Van theo rubric.
- Tao study plan 30/60/90 ngay.
- Hien viec can hoc hom nay.
- Tieu chi xong: hoc sinh nhan feedback bai viet va lich hoc ca nhan.

## Week 7: Analytics va Gamification

- Dashboard tien bo, heatmap topic, streak, XP, badges.
- Predicted score range theo du lieu attempt.
- Tieu chi xong: dashboard co insight hanh dong ro rang.

## Week 8: QA, Content Review, Pilot

- Kiem thu responsive, accessibility, performance.
- Giao vien review mau cau hoi.
- Pilot voi nhom 20-50 hoc sinh.
- Tieu chi xong: MVP san sang demo va co backlog sau pilot.

## Ranh Gioi MVP

- Uu tien web responsive.
- Dung AI cho tutor va Van grading, chua can voice.
- Noi dung AI-generated phai qua review truoc khi dung trong goi tra phi.
