# Bo Dac Ta Ung Dung On Thi Tuyen Sinh Lop 10 TP.HCM

Thu muc nay la nen tang san pham cho ung dung web/mobile on thi tuyen sinh lop 10 TP.HCM. Noi dung duoc tong hop tu `Mota.txt`, cac prompt mon Van/Anh lien quan va cau truc de thi TP.HCM gan day.

Cap nhat theo cau truc TP.HCM tu nam 2025/2026: Toan 7 bai theo GDPT 2018; Ngu van 2 phan tich hop doc hieu va viet; Tieng Anh 4 phan, 40 cau. Du lieu mau la AI-generated theo ma tran, khong sao chep de chinh thuc.
