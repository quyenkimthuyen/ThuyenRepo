# Product Requirements

## Tam Nhin

Xay dung ung dung on thi tuyen sinh lop 10 TP.HCM giup hoc sinh lop 9 hoc co lo trinh, luyen de sat thuc te va nhan phan hoi ca nhan hoa tu AI. San pham uu tien hieu qua hoc tap hon giai tri hoa qua muc.

## Muc Tieu Hoc Tap

- He thong hoa kien thuc Toan, Ngu van, Tieng Anh theo chuong trinh THCS, trong tam lop 8-9 va lop 9.
- Phat hien lo hong kien thuc bang bai kiem tra dau vao va lich su lam bai.
- Tao lo trinh 30/60/90 ngay theo diem hien tai, diem muc tieu, truong mong muon va thoi gian hoc moi ngay.
- Luyen cau hoi theo chuyen de, muc do va ma tran de TP.HCM.
- Luyen de trong moi truong gan thi that: dem nguoc, khong goi y, nop bai, cham va phan tich.
- Theo doi tien bo va du doan khoang diem thi that theo du lieu hoc tap.

## Persona

### Hoc sinh mat goc

- Muc tieu: dau nguyen vong phu hop, tang tu 3-5 len 6-7 diem.
- Can: ly thuyet ngan, bai mau tung buoc, goi y nhieu, nhac on lap lai.
- Rui ro: de bo cuoc neu gap qua nhieu cau kho.

### Hoc sinh trung binh kha

- Muc tieu: 7-8 diem, tranh mat diem o cau quen.
- Can: luyen dang lap lai, phan tich loi sai, de mo phong dinh ky.
- Rui ro: hoc lan man, thieu ke hoach.

### Hoc sinh kha gioi

- Muc tieu: 8.5-10 diem, tang kha nang cau phan loai.
- Can: cau van dung cao, chien luoc thoi gian, loi giai toi uu.
- Rui ro: chu quan voi cau co ban, thieu ky nang trinh bay.

## Pham Vi MVP

- Web responsive cho hoc sinh.
- Dang nhap email/Google.
- On tap 3 mon voi topic, lesson, question bank.
- Luyen cau theo topic va thi thu theo ma tran.
- AI gia su 3 che do: Hoc cung thay co, Tu luyen, Thi that.
- Dashboard ca nhan: diem, ty le dung, chuoi ngay hoc, chuyen de yeu.
- Seed du lieu 2.100 cau AI-generated.

## Ngoai Pham Vi MVP

- Ung dung mobile native.
- Marketplace giao vien.
- Thanh toan goi hoc.
- Cham noi/phat am bang audio thoi gian thuc.
- Sao chep nguyen de chinh thuc co ban quyen.

## Nguyen Tac Su Pham

- Khong dua dap an ngay trong che do tu luyen; uu tien cau hoi goi mo.
- Phan hoi theo loi sai cu the, khong chi noi dung/sai.
- Ngu van khong khuyen khich hoc thuoc van mau; chi dua dan y, tieu chi, goi y dien dat.
- Gamification phuc vu ky luat hoc tap, khong tao man hinh gay nghien.
- Moi cau hoi can co loi giai va meo lam bai de hoc sinh tu sua.

## Chi So Thanh Cong

- 70% hoc sinh hoan thanh onboarding va bai diagnostic.
- 50% hoc sinh quay lai hoc trong 7 ngay dau.
- Moi hoc sinh lam toi thieu 120 cau trong thang dau.
- Ty le cai thien diem thi thu trung binh sau 4 tuan >= 0.8 diem.
- 90% cau hoi seed co metadata hop le va duoc giao vien duyet truoc khi public.
