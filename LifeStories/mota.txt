# PROJECT: Life Choices

Tagline:

"Every Choice Shapes You"

---

# PRODUCT VISION

Build an interactive story-based web application that helps users develop better thinking patterns, communication skills, emotional intelligence, and life judgment through immersive branching stories.

Users should never feel:

"I am being tested."

Users should feel:

"I am inside a story."

The system learns about the user through choices made during story progression.

---

# CORE PHILOSOPHY

People rarely change because of advice.

People change because of experiences.

The application should teach through:

* Stories
* Dialogue
* Consequences
* Relationships
* Reflection

Never through lectures.

Never through direct moral teaching.

---

# LEARNING MODEL

Instead of:

Question
↓
Answer
↓
Analysis

Use:

Story
↓
Dialogue Choice
↓
Consequence
↓
Story Continues
↓
Pattern Recognition

# STORY SYSTEM

Stories are the heart of the platform.

Each story contains:

* Characters
* Dialogues
* Choices
* Branches
* Consequences

Stories should feel like:

Interactive drama

not educational content.

---

# STORY STRUCTURE

Each story consists of scenes.

Example:

Scene 1

Friend asks to copy homework.

Dialogue appears.

Player chooses response.

Scene 2

Friend reacts.

Scene 3

Teacher discovers issue.

Scene 4

Long-term consequence appears.

---

# DIALOGUE CHOICES

Every scene should provide 2-5 choices.

Example:

Friend:

"Can I copy your homework?"

Choice A:

"Sure."

Choice B:

"No."

Choice C:

"I'll help you understand it."

Choice D:

"Maybe next time."

Every choice should have:

* Pros
* Cons
* Hidden consequences

Avoid obvious good/bad choices.

---

# CONSEQUENCE SYSTEM

The most important feature.

Consequences may appear:

Immediately

OR

Much later.

Example:

User lies.

No immediate punishment.

Five scenes later:

Trust decreases.

Relationship changes.

Future opportunities disappear.

Users should naturally learn cause and effect.

---

# CHARACTER RELATIONSHIP SYSTEM

Track relationships with NPCs.

Example:

{
friendship: 80,
trust: 60,
respect: 50,
dependency: 30
}

Choices affect relationship values.

---

# PERSONALITY TRACKING

Users should never see this directly during gameplay.

System tracks:

{
ownership,
empathy,
assertiveness,
responsibility,
curiosity,
discipline,
integrity,
courage,
resilience,
cooperation
}

Each choice contributes to hidden personality traits.

---

# THINKING PATTERN ENGINE

Every choice contains tags.

Example:

Choice A:

Tags:

* avoid_conflict
* short_term_gain

Choice B:

Tags:

* assertiveness
* responsibility

Choice C:

Tags:

* empathy
* teaching

System accumulates these tags over time.

---

# LIFE PROFILE

After multiple stories.

Generate insight reports.

Never judge.

Never label users negatively.

Use neutral observations.

Example:

"You often prioritize maintaining harmony."

"You frequently take responsibility in difficult situations."

"You tend to avoid direct confrontation."

Provide observations only.

# SUCCESS METRIC

The application succeeds when users say:

"I didn't realize I usually react this way."

instead of:

"I learned a lesson."

The platform should reveal thinking patterns through story experiences rather than direct teaching.

Nếu là tôi, sau prompt này tôi sẽ yêu cầu Cursor làm thêm một phiên bản MVP nhỏ hơn nữa:

* Chỉ 1 giai đoạn: THPT.
* Chỉ 5 câu chuyện.
* Mỗi câu chuyện 10–15 cảnh.
* Có hệ thống quan hệ và hậu quả trễ.

Làm được MVP đó trước. Nếu người chơi thực sự bị cuốn vào câu chuyện và muốn chơi tiếp, bạn đã chứng minh được ý tưởng cốt lõi trước khi xây hàng trăm câu chuyện. Điều khó nhất của dự án này sẽ không phải code, mà là **thiết kế những câu chuyện đủ chân thật để người chơi thấy chính mình trong đó**.
